About Me

The Designer Behind The Pixels

I'm Hafiz Moeen Raza — a WordPress & UI/UX Designer based in Pakistan, designing premium digital experiences for clients across the USA, UAE, Europe, and beyond.

Over the past 5+ years, I've built real estate portals, eCommerce stores, and corporate websites that don't just look beautiful — they're engineered to convert visitors into customers, generate leads, and drive measurable results.

My foundation comes from formal design training at SICS Institute, a PSDF Certified website design program, and UI/UX prototyping through the E-Hunar Program — giving me a full-spectrum perspective from wireframe to live deployment.

Today, I also integrate AI chatbots (ChatGPT & Claude APIs) into WordPress sites — building intelligent, always-on assistants trained on your business, your brand, and your goals.
